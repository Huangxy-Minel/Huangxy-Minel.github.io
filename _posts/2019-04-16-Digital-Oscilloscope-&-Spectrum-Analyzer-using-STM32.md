## Electronic design competition experience

This is the first time I have participated in an electronic design competition. The competition requires the inductive current method to measure the signal waveform and spectrum. So, when I was a freshman, I started the journey of learning signals. Writing the FFT algorithm for the first time can be said to be full of errors. So after finishing the signal and system, I re-written the FFT algorithm and improved it energy spectrum calibration algorithm, which improved spectral resolution by 80% (4096-point FFT, 20kHz sampling rate, resolution increased from 5Hz to 1Hz).
